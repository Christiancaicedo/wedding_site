Things to Do

Looking for things to do while in Austin? We've picked our favorite spots around town. Surprise! They're pretty much all food related!

Jester King Brewery

Jester King is hands down our favorite brewery in Austin. They have many varieties of breer on top ranging from ales, porters, IPA and even their own meed! On top of the fantastic beers they brew on location, they have one of the best selections of handcrafted pizza. The property sits on 165 acres with a got farm and a beatiful 2 mile hiking loop around the brewery itself. This is the best place to get drinks, food and a little fresh air!

Check Out Jester King

Zilker Park

Looking to get outside along a beautiful skyline of Austin? Look no further than Zilker Park! Zilker Park is the most famous park in Austin which accounts for over 350 acres of land right in the heart of downtown Austin. Walk along the green belt, check out the botanical gardens, swim in Barton Springs, or kayak/paddleboard along the Colorado River in downtown. Zilker is a great place to take friends, family and the dog to hangout and relax!

Check Out Zilker

Perry's Friday Porkchop

The famous Perry's Porkchop is one of our favorite meals for a reason. It is downright AMAZING! Pery's has spent over 40 years perfecting their porkchop recipe. The porkchiop is cured, roasted, slow smoked carmelized and topped with their herb-garlic butter. This is one lunch you DO NOT want to miss out on! However, in order to get this deliscious meal without breaking the bank, you need to make a reservation for lunch on Friday's only! Be sure to dress nice for this special occasion!

Make Your Reservation Now!

Phoebe's Brunch

Looking for a nice brunch meal after too much fun from the wedding? Come hangout at Phoebe's! Phoebe's is hands down our favorite brunch location in Austin. Phoebe's has all of our favorite Texas brunch meals from Crab & Crawfish Benny to Chicken Fried Steak. Enjoy your meal with a local Texas brew and relax while you recover from the night before!

Reserve Your Brunch

Louie's Craft BBQ Food Truck

Located just 5 minutes from our new home off the small Buda Main Street, Louie's has some of the best BBQ in town. Don't be fooled by their small food truck. There is nothing small about their portions or flavor! We have spent the better half of 7 years locating our favorite BBQ in town and were lucky enough to find it in our own backyard. Enjoy yourself some incredible smoked brisket or dive into their giant pulled pork sandwiches made on fresh briouche buns!

Get Some BBQ!
